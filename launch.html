A podcast about children's TV and the brilliant people who make it happen. 

TEAM

Billie Collins is a writer from the Wirral. She's written scripts for stage, screen and audio, with work currently in development with Box of Tricks Theatre Company. She's also previously interviewed filmmakers for BFI NETWORK, hosted panel discussions for the BFI Film Academy in partnership with the BBC, curated online events at the London Film Festival 2020 and makes a mean cup of tea. Find her on @_billiecollins

Clodagh Chapman is a writer, theatre maker and filmmaker. Haivng previously run panels at the Almeida Theatre and the London Metropolitan Archives, she is now a Trainee at Toasted Productions. She can be found over at @CloChpmn on Twitter and Instagram.
