ABOUT

WHAT'S THIS ABOUT THEN?

Even Baddies Wear Helmets is a podcast all about the craft of making children’s television, in conversation with the grown-ups who make it happen. Throughout the series, host Billie Collins chats to the sector’s top producers, writers, directors, and actors about everything from adapting children’s books to voiceover for animation. Kids’ telly is home to some of the most forward thinking, innovative and exciting content – and we’re here to find out how it’s made.

WHAT DOES THE TITLE MEAN?

The title ‘Even Baddies Wear Helmets’ comes from what (we were once told!) is a list of rules for writing preschool shows. Basically, because you don’t want kids copying dangerous behaviour, no matter how bad your baddie is, they will always wear a cycle helmet when they take to the roads. For us, it sums up all the best things about kids’ TV – it’s humour, playfulness, and lack of cynicism. (It also sounds cooler than ‘The Kids’ TV Podcast.’)

TEAM

BILLIE COLLINS - HOST

Billie Collins is a writer from the Wirral. She's written scripts for stage, screen and audio, with work currently in development with Box of Tricks and ThickSkin. She's also previously interviewed filmmakers for BFI NETWORK, hosted panel discussions for the BFI Film Academy in partnership with the BBC, curated online events at the London Film Festival 2020 and makes a mean cup of tea.

CLODAGH CHAPMAN - PRODUCER

Clodagh Chapman is a theatre maker and filmmaker based in Manchester, whose debut short made the Official Selection of BIFA- and BAFTA-qualifying festivals. She also freelances as a journalist and panel host, where she's previously curated and hosted events for BFI London Film Festival, run panels for the Almeida Theatre, and written for Bristol 24/7. She also makes a cracking mushroom risotto.

FINLAY STAFFORD - COMPOSER

Finlay Stafford is a composer, drummer and musician from Sheffield.

LUCY TILLER - GRAPHIC DESIGNER

Lucy is a campaigns worker based in London, UK. Alongside this, she works as a freelance illustrator.
